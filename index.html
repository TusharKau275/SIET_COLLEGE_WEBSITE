About SIET Nilokheri

Shaping Tomorrow's Engineers Since 2016

State Institute of Engineering and Technology, Nilokheri, is a premier government-aided technical institution in Karnal district, Haryana. Since 1954, the institute has produced thousands of skilled engineers who have made their mark globally.

Affiliated to MDU Rohtak and approved by AICTE, SIET offers comprehensive B.Tech programs across various engineering disciplines with a focus on holistic student development.

  • 🏛️

    AICTE & MDU Approved

    Fully accredited with recognized degree programs

  • 🔬

    Research Infrastructure

    Well-equipped labs and dedicated research centers

  • 📚

    Comprehensive Library

    Books, journals, digital resources & e-library access

  • 🏃

    Sports & Cultural

    Vibrant campus life with full sports facilities
